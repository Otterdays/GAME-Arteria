## 7. Skilling Equipment & Synergies
*Worn or used by the player to buff the Smithing loop.*
- **Hammer** (Base tool)
- **Resonance Hammer / Imcando Hammer** (Equippable, saves inventory space, grants buffs).
- **Ammo Moulds / Multi-Moulds** (Required for Cannonballs/Nails, increases yield).
- **Ring of Forging** (Bypasses Iron smelting failure rate).
- **Goldsmith Gauntlets** (Massive XP boost for Gold/Electrum).
- **Thermal Gauntlets** (Required to handle Volatile / Cosmic metals without taking damage).
- **Smiths' Uniform / Varrock Armour** (Provides % chance to smelt 2x bars or smith 1-tick faster).
- **Coal Bag / Dust Pouch** (Stores extra fuel for massive furnace runs).
- **Ice Gloves** (Instantly cools super-heated items).
