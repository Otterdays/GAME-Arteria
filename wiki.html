Welcome to Valdoria

Arteria is a cosmic fantasy idle RPG that blends magical wonder with eldritch dark fantasy. As an Anchor, a soul of unusual cosmic weight, you are the only one capable of surviving the Unraveling—a chaotic glitch in reality that transforms ordinary sheep into abstract geometry and makes gravity take a holiday.

This wiki contains the comprehensive records of the game's mechanics, the history of the Divine Sneeze, and guides on how to optimize your journey toward cosmic mastery.

Core Game Loop

Success in Arteria hinges on the cycle of training and efficiency:

  1. Gathering: Mine ore, chop logs, fish deep waters, and scavenge debris.
  2. Crafting: Smelt bars, forge equipment, and brew powerful elixirs.
  3. Progressing: Unlock new tiers of content, complete story quests, and level up to 99 across all skills.
  4. Idle Efficiency: Your progress continues even while you sleep, capped at 24 hours for F2P players and 7 days for Patrons.

Random Events & Skill Pets

Arteria features a dynamic Random Event System that directly interacts with your Skilling. Each successful "tick" (when you gain an item or XP) has a chance to trigger various cosmic anomalies, blessings, or attract a rare wandering companion.

The Event Pool

During active or offline skilling, you have a baseline 0.5% chance per successful tick to trigger a Random Event. A minimum cooldown of 60 ticks prevents event spamming.

Blibbertooth's Blessing
Provides a massive burst of bonus XP. Amount = Active Skill Level × 5.
Genie's Gift
A classic lamp offering. Grants XP to a chosen skill equal to Level × 10.
Treasure Chest
Grants a cache of gold. Base 25 + (Skill Level × 8).
Lucky Strike
Instantly multiplies the XP gained on the current tick by 2x.
Cosmic Sneeze
Duplicates your gathered resources when a reality-bending sneeze occurs.
Goblin Peek
Your first enemy sighting (flavor event) pointing toward future combat.

Skill Pets

Every single training action carries a tiny 0.01% chance (1 in 10,000) to drop a permanent, equippable Skill Pet associated with the skill being trained. Earning one is a mark of true dedication.

  • 🪨 Rocky: A small, weirdly smooth rock. (Mining)
  • 🪵 Timber: A tiny sapling that yells "Timber!". (Logging)
  • 🫧 Bubbles: A fish that floats in the air. (Fishing)

  • 🔥 Sparks: A tiny, harmless flame. (Smithing)

  • 🔨 Clank: A very dense, miniature anvil. (Forging)

Geography of Valdoria

Valdoria is a floating peninsula kingdom roughly the size of Ireland, surrounded by the Shimmering Sea and the endless void beyond. It is the last bastion of stability in an unraveling cosmos.

The Six Major Regions

  • The Crownlands: The central hub and location of the player's home and the main market.
  • Frostvale: A snowy wonder available during the Voidmas season (December).
  • Whispering Woods: An enchanted forest where the trees actually gossip about your training speed.
  • Fey Markets: A planar crossover point where otherworldly traders offer bizarre artifacts.
  • Scorched Reach: A volcanic wasteland pooling with Voidmire essence. Level 40 required.
  • Skyward Peaks: High-altitude mountains that touch the lower Celestial Spires. Level 70 required.

The Unraveling

Each location has a Corruption Level from 0% to 100%. As the Unraveling spreads, physics cease to function correctly. Community cleansing events are required to keep the collapse from becoming permanent.

Lore & Narrative

The Cosmic Sneeze

The universe was not created by a grand design, but by an accident. Blibbertooth the Unserious, a being of pure cosmic energy, felt a tickle while observing the primordial void. The resulting sneeze birthed our reality.

This is why magic is unpredictable, why chickens exist, and why the universe doesn't take itself too seriously. However, the Cult of the Serious believes this was a mistake. Led by "The Accountant," they seek to balance the cosmic books and eliminate all absurdity—which would effectively delete existence as we know it.
