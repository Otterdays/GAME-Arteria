Architecture

Core Systems

Arteria is a fully modular monorepo. Each layer of the game is a self-contained, independently testable system.

⚙️
arteria-engine
Game Engine

A pure TypeScript package with zero React dependencies. Houses the RuneScape-style XP table, the delta-time tick processor (TickSystem), and the GameEngine orchestrator that handles both realtime and offline batch processing. Independently unit-tested with Jest.

XPTable.ts TickSystem.ts GameEngine.ts jest-tested
🏦
arteria-state
Redux Store

The single source of truth for all game state. Built with Redux Toolkit's gameSlice, managing player skills, inventory, active tasks, and gold. Fully typed with RTK and react-redux hooks. Wired into the UI layer via typed useAppSelector / useAppDispatch.

RTK 2.11 gameSlice typed hooks
💾
arteria-persist
MMKV Persistence

Synchronous C++ key-value storage powered by react-native-mmkv v4. The usePersistence hook listens to React Native's AppState. On background, it snapshots Redux state + timestamp to MMKV. On foreground, it loads the save, calculates elapsed time, and hydrates the store.

MMKV v4 AppState autosave
⏱️
arteria-loop
Game Loop

useGameLoop drives the realtime experience — a 100ms setInterval that feeds delta-time into the engine and dispatches results to Redux. On first load it calculates offline catch-up via a timestamp diff, capped at 24 hours for F2P fairness.

100ms tick offline catchup 24h F2P cap
📱
arteria-ui
Mobile App

Built on Expo SDK 55 with Expo Router v6 for file-based navigation. Features a dark Melvor-inspired theme with 30+ design tokens, 5 tab screens (Skills, Combat, Bank, Shop, Settings), skill cards with animated XP bars, and real-time train/stop dispatch buttons.

Expo SDK 55 Expo Router RN 0.83 New Arch
🧪
arteria-tests
Test Suite

Multi-project Jest at monorepo root. Unit tests for XPTable (RuneScape XP curve), TickSystem (partial tick carryover, RNG, offline batching), and GameEngine (createNewPlayer, processOffline). npm run test:coverage for engine coverage. Integration and E2E placeholders. Target: 80% on business logic.

The Order of the Balanced Scale
Led by a sentient suit of empty armor. Perfectly normal.
🌑
The Void-Touched Collective
Not evil — just unsettling vibes and excellent parties.
📋
The Celestial Bureaucracy
Angels who require divine blessings in triplicate.
🤡
Cult of Blibbertooth

Wandering Souls

Companions

Hire Wandering Souls — NPCs with their own skills who train, fight, and occasionally cause explosions on your behalf.

⚔️
Barnaby the Uncertain
Warrior · Unlocks at Leadership 20
A well-meaning warrior who deals devastating damage — when he doesn't accidentally hit himself. Optimistic regardless of outcomes.
Trait: 2× damage, but 50% chance to strike himself instead. He calls this "character building."
🧪
Scholar Yvette
Researcher · Unlocks at Leadership 35
A brilliant researcher who dramatically speeds up your crafting queues. Minor side effect: occasional lab explosions.
Trait: +30% crafting speed. 5% chance per craft to explode — but you get a partial resource refund and a funny quote.
🛡️
Sir Reginald Pomp
Knight · Unlocks at Leadership 50
An aristocratic knight who automatically sells your grey-tier loot. He delivers a long, eloquent speech about each discarded item.
Trait: Auto-sells junk items for gold. You can't skip the speeches. You learn to love them.
Protagonist

The Anchor

You aren't just a randomly chosen hero. You are a cosmic accident—an Anchor whose soul is unusually heavy, immune to the chaos of the Unraveling.

🧬
Ancestries
Play as a standard Human, a time-dilated Elf, a cosmic-mining Dwarf, a partially unreal Fey-Touched, or a translucent Void-Blooded character.
⚖️
Affinities
Align yourself with defensive Luminar energy, offensive Voidmire corruption, or walk the slow but perfectly flexible path of the Balanced.
🃏
Cosmic Quirks
Roll a random comedic modifier on creation. You might attract sentient furniture, or find your hair changing color when someone lies nearby.
Combat System

The Encounter

Combat occurs in pocket dimensions where time moves differently, allowing offline progression. Auto-combat learns your preferences and auto-flees to save your loot.

⚔️
Quick Hits
Delves

Short 5-10 minute active experiences, or 1-hour offline auto-clears. Perfect for a quick lunch break or queuing up right before a meeting.

🗺️
Deep Dives
Expeditions

Multi-floor gauntlets spanning up to 30 minutes active or 4 hours offline. Prepare your loadout and limited consumables wisely before heading in.

🌀
Weekly Rules
Cosmic Modifiers

Dungeons rotate modifiers weekly. "Gravity's Suggestion" reverses fall damage (falling up hurts). "Blibbertooth's Blessing" makes enemies explode into confetti on crits.

Lifestyle

A Day in Arteria

Designed to respect your time. Progress happens whether you play for 2 minutes or 2 hours.

9:00 AM — Morning Commute (2 min)
📱 Collect & Queue
Open the "While You Were Away" report. Grab overnight combat loot. Start a 2-hour crafting queue. Check daily quests.
12:30 PM — Lunch Break (15 min)
⛏️ Active Play
Mine 10 Silver Ore for a daily quest. Run a dungeon in active mode for bonus loot. Collect finished crafts and restart the queue.
6:00 PM — Evening Session (45 min)
⚔️ Deep Play
Test a new combat ability. Visit the Fey Market for rare recipes. Start an 8-hour legendary craft. Harvest your garden.
10:00 PM — Bedtime (3 min)
🌙 Queue Overnight
Set auto-combat in a new area. Queue long crafts. Set a push notification for when your Fey-Touched Sword finishes. Sleep well.
